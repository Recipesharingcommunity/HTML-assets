INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 cup ghee or oil
  • 1/2 cup lukewarm water
  • 1 egg, beaten (optional)

PREPARATION

  • Mix flour, salt, sugar, and baking powder.
  • Add ghee or oil; mix until crumbly.
  • Gradually add water; knead dough.
  • Rest dough for 30 minutes.
  • Divide into 6-8 portions.
  • Roll each portion into ball.
  • Flatten into thin circles.
  • Fold and twist dough to form layers.
  • Cook on hot tava or griddle.
  • Brush with ghee or oil.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups parotta (or flour to make parotta)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder, Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil, Chopped cilantro (optional)

PREPARATION

  • Cook parotta; tear into small pieces.
  • Beat eggs; scramble in pan.
  • Add onion, garlic, ginger paste; sauté.
  • Add cumin seeds, coriander powder, turmeric powder, chili powder; stir-fry.
  • Combine scrambled eggs, parotta pieces.
  • Mix well; add salt.
  • Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Garnish with cilantro (if using).
  • Serve hot.

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g chicken, boneless, cut into small pieces
  • 2 medium onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 cups water

PREPARATION

  • Heat oil; sauté onions, garlic, ginger paste.
  • Add chicken; cook until browned.
  • Add cumin seeds, coriander powder, turmeric powder, chili powder, garam masala.
  • Stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  • Add water; cook until chicken is tender.
  • Season with salt.
