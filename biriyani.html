INGREDIENTS

  • 1 kg mutton, cut into small pieces, 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp ghee/oil, 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds, 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder, 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp salt, 2 cups water
  • 2 onions, chopped, 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger paste, 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Chopped cilantro (optional)

PREPARATION

  • Marinate mutton in yogurt, lemon juice, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, salt for 30 minutes.
  • Heat ghee/oil, add cumin seeds, coriander seeds.
  • Sauté onions, garlic, ginger paste.
  • Add marinated mutton, cook until tender.
  • Cook basmati rice separately.
  • Layer rice, mutton mixture.
  • Steam until cooked.
  • Garnish with cilantro (optional).

INGREDIENTS

  • 4-6 eggs, hard-boiled and diced, 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoons ghee/oil, 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds, 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder, 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt, to taste, 2 cups water
  • 1 onion, chopped, 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste, Chopped cilantro (optional)

PREPARATION

  • Cook basmati rice and set aside.
  • Heat ghee/oil, add cumin seeds and coriander seeds.
  • Sauté onion, garlic and ginger paste.
  • Add turmeric, chili powder, garam masala and salt.
  • Add diced eggs and mix well.
  • Layer cooked rice and egg mixture.
  • Steam until combined.
  • Garnish with cilantro (optional).

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice – 1 cup (washed), Tomatoes – 2 (chopped), Onion – 1 (sliced)
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 1 tsp, Spices: Turmeric, chili powder, garam masala – 1 tsp each
  • Salt – to taste
  • Oil – 1 tbsp
  • Water – 2 cups
  • Coriander leaves – for garnish

PREPARATION

  • Sauté: Heat oil, fry onions until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste.
  • Cook tomatoes: Add chopped tomatoes, spices, and salt. Cook until soft.
  • Add rice: Mix in rice and water. Cover and cook for 15 minutes on low heat.
  • Garnish & serve: Fluff the rice, garnish with coriander, and enjoy your quick tomato biryani!

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice: 1/3 cup basmati rice
  • Mixed Vegetables: 1/2 cup (chopped)
  • Onion: 1/4 small, sliced
  • Tomato: 1/4 small, chopped
  • Spices: 1 clove garlic, 1/2-inch ginger, 1/2 green chili, 1/2 tsp biryani masala, salt, cumin seeds, turmeric, 1 tbsp oil, 1 cup water

PREPARATION

  • Rinse & Soak: Wash rice until clear, soak for 20 minutes, then drain.
  • Cook Base: Heat oil, add cumin and onions until golden, then garlic, ginger, and chili. Cook for 1 minute.
  • Add Veggies: Stir in tomato and mixed veggies; cook for 3-4 minutes.
  • Cook Rice: Add water, turmeric, masala, and salt. Boil, then add rice and cook until 70% done (about 5 minutes).
  • Steam: Cover and cook on low for 10 minutes until fully cooked. Fluff and serve!

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice: 1/3 cup basmati rice
  • Mixed Vegetables: 1/2 cup (carrots, peas, etc.)
  • Onion: 1/4 small, sliced
  • Tomato: 1/4 small, chopped
  • Spices: 1 clove garlic, 1/2-inch ginger, 1/2 green chili, 1/2 tsp biryani masala, 1/4 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp cumin seeds, salt, 1 tbsp oil, 1 cup water

PREPARATION

  • Rinse & Soak: Wash the rice until clear; soak for 20 minutes and drain.
  • Sauté: Heat oil, add cumin and sliced onion; cook until golden brown.
  • Add Aromatics: Stir in garlic, ginger, and green chili; cook for 1 minute. Add tomato and mixed veggies; cook for 3-4 minutes.
  • Cook Rice: Add water, turmeric, biryani masala, and salt. Boil, then add rice; cook until 70% done (about 5 minutes).
  • Steam: Cover and cook on low for 10 minutes until fully cooked. Fluff and serve!
