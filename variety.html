INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 3-4 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

PREPARATION

  • Heat oil, add cumin seeds.
  • Sauté onion, garlic, and tomatoes.
  • Add coriander powder and salt.
  • Mix with cooked rice.
  • Garnish with cilantro.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 cups water

PREPARATION

  • Heat ghee in a pan.
  • Add cumin seeds, coriander seeds.
  • Sauté until fragrant.
  • Add rice, salt.
  • Mix well.
  • Add water.
  • Cook until rice is tender.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tsp lemon juice (optional)

PREPARATION

  • Heat coconut oil in a pan.
  • Add cumin seeds, coriander seeds.
  • Sauté until fragrant.
  • Add grated coconut.
  • Cook until lightly toasted.
  • Mix with cooked rice.
  • Season with salt.
  • Add lemon juice (if using).

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1/2 cup tamarind paste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Chopped cilantro (for garnish)

PREPARATION

  • Heat oil in a pan.
  • Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds.
  • Sauté until fragrant.
  • Add tamarind paste, coriander powder.
  • Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Mix with cooked rice.
  • Season with salt.
  • Garnish with cilantro.
