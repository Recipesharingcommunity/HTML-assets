INGREDIENTS

  • 200g prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 200g noodles (rice or egg)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped scallions and lemon wedges for garnish

PREPARATION

  • Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  • Heat oil in a wok or large skillet.
  • Add onion, garlic, and ginger; stir-fry until onion is translucent.
  • Add prawns; stir-fry until pink.
  • Add soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and tomato sauce; stir-fry.
  • Combine cooked noodles and prawn mixture.
  • Season with salt and pepper.

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g noodles (egg or rice)
  • 250g boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (e.g., cabbage, carrots, bell peppers)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon chicken seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

PREPARATION

  • Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  • Heat oil in a wok or large skillet.
  • Add onion and garlic; stir-fry until onion is translucent.
  • Add chicken; cook until browned and cooked through.
  • Add mixed vegetables; stir-fry until tender.
  • Add cooked noodles, soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and chicken seasoning; stir-fry.
  • Season with salt and pepper.
  • Serve hot, garnished with chopped scallions.

INGREDIENTS

  • 250g pasta (e.g., penne, fusilli, or macaroni)
  • 2 cups grated cheese (e.g., cheddar, mozzarella, parmesan)
  • 1 cup grated ricotta cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: garlic, herbs (e.g., basil, parsley), ham or bacon

PREPARATION

  • Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  • Melt butter in a saucepan.
  • Add flour; whisk to make a roux.
  • Gradually add milk; whisk until smooth.
  • Add grated cheese; stir until melted.
  • Combine cooked pasta, cheese sauce, and ricotta cheese (if using).
  • Season with salt, pepper, and optional ingredients.
  • Transfer to baking dish; top with extra cheese.
  • Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.

INGREDIENTS

  • 250g pasta (e.g., spaghetti, linguine, fettuccine)
  • 200g prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley or basil for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

PREPARATION

  • Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  • Heat olive oil in a large skillet.
  • Add garlic; sauté for 1 minute.
  • Add prawns; cook until pink (2-3 minutes per side).
  • Add lemon zest, lemon juice, and paprika.
  • Toss cooked pasta with prawn mixture.
  • Season with salt and pepper.
  • Garnish with parsley or basil.
  • Serve with Parmesan cheese (if using).

  • Arrabbiata Sauce: add red pepper flakes for spice

  • Alfredo Sauce: use butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese
