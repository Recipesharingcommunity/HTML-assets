INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1 cup boneless, skinless chicken breast, diced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (e.g., peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

PREPARATION

  • Heat oil in a large skillet or wok.
  • Add chicken and cook until browned.
  • Add onion and garlic; stir-fry until tender.
  • Add mixed vegetables; stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  • Push ingredients aside; scramble eggs.
  • Mix eggs with other ingredients.
  • Add cooked rice; stir-fry for 5 minutes.
  • Add soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using); stir-fry.
  • Season with salt and pepper.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (e.g., peas, carrots, corn)
  • 1 cup chopped bell peppers (any color)
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: scallions, broccoli, mushrooms

PREPARATION

  • Heat oil in a large skillet or wok.
  • Add onion and garlic; stir-fry until tender.
  • Add mixed vegetables and bell peppers; stir-fry for 3 minutes.
  • Add cooked rice; stir-fry for 5 minutes.
  • Add soy sauce; stir-fry.
  • Season with salt and pepper.
  • Add optional ingredients (if using).
  • Serve hot.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1 cup prawns (shrimp), peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (e.g., peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: scallions, egg

PREPARATION

  • Heat oil in a large skillet or wok.
  • Add prawns; cook until pink.
  • Remove prawns; set aside.
  • Add onion and garlic; stir-fry until tender.
  • Add mixed vegetables; stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  • Add cooked rice; stir-fry for 5 minutes.
  • Add soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using); stir-fry.
  • Return prawns; stir-fry.
  • Season with salt and pepper.
  • Add optional ingredients (if using).
  • Serve hot.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1 cup mixed mushrooms (button, shiitake, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
