Chicken 65

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Chopped parsley or thyme for garnish

PREPARATION

  • Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  • Mix minced garlic, olive oil, salt, black pepper, paprika, and cayenne pepper (if using).
  • Place chicken breasts in a baking dish.
  • Brush garlic mixture evenly onto both sides of chicken.
  • Bake for 25-30 minutes or until cooked through.
  • Garnish with chopped parsley or thyme.
  • Serve hot.

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 2 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • Salt, to taste
  • Chopped parsley, for garnish

PREPARATION

  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Mix lemon juice, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and melted butter.
  • Season chicken with salt.
  • Place chicken in a baking dish; brush with lemon mixture.
  • Bake for 25-30 minutes or until cooked through.
  • Garnish with chopped parsley.
  • Serve hot.

  • 1 tsp garlic powder (optional)
  • 1 tsp paprika (optional)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder (optional)
  • 1 tsp paprika (optional)

PREPARATION

  • Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  • Mix olive oil, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and paprika (if using).
  • Brush mixture evenly onto both sides of chicken.
  • Grill chicken 5-7 minutes per side or until cooked through.
  • Let rest 5 minutes before serving.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb chicken (cut into bite-sized pieces)
  • 2 onions (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp tomato ketchup
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil (for frying)

PREPARATION

  • Heat oil in a pan.
  • Add onions and garlic; fry until tender.
  • Add chicken; cook until browned.
  • Add chilli flakes, soy sauce, and tomato ketchup.
  • Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  • Season with salt and pepper.
  • Serve hot.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb boneless chicken (cut into small pieces)
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • Oil (for frying)

PREPARATION

  • Mix flour, salt, chili powder, garam masala, ginger paste, and garlic paste.
  • Add yogurt and lemon juice; mix well.
  • Add chicken; marinate for 30 minutes.
  • Fry chicken in hot oil until golden brown.
  • Drain excess oil.
  • Serve hot.
