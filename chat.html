INGREDIENTS

  • Bread slices – 2
  • Mixed veggies (tomato, cucumber, onion) – sliced
  • Cheese slice – 1 (optional)
  • Butter – for grilling
  • Salt & pepper – to taste

PREPARATION

  • Layer veggies: Place veggies and cheese between the bread slices.
  • Season: Sprinkle salt and pepper.
  • Grill: Butter the outside of the sandwich and grill until golden brown.
  • Serve: Cut and enjoy your quick, healthy sandwich!

INGREDIENTS

  • Bread slices – 2
  • 1 egg – scrambled or fried
  • Mixed veggies (tomato, onion, cucumber) – sliced
  • Butter – for grilling
  • Salt & pepper – to taste

PREPARATION

  • Assemble: Place veggies and cooked egg between bread slices.

  • Serve: Cut and enjoy your hearty sandwich!

INGREDIENTS

  • Brown bread slices – 2
  • Boiled corn – ¼ cup
  • Grated cheese – ¼ cup
  • Butter – for grilling
  • Salt & pepper – to taste

PREPARATION

  • Mix filling: Combine boiled corn, grated cheese, salt, and pepper.
  • Assemble: Spread the filling between bread slices.
  • Grill: Butter the outside and grill until golden brown.
  • Serve: Slice and enjoy your cheesy, crunchy sandwich!

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups flour, 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder, 1/4 cup ghee/oil
  • 1/2 cup water, 1 cup peas
  • 1 cup potatoes, boiled/mashed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds, Salt, to taste

PREPARATION

  • Mix flour, salt, baking powder.
  • Add ghee/oil, water; knead.
  • Rest 30 minutes.
  • Heat oil; add cumin seeds, onion, peas, potatoes.
  • Mix well.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup semolina
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 cup ghee/oil, 1/2 cup lukewarm water
  • For filling: boiled chickpeas, potatoes, onions, Pani (spicy water)
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Chopped cilantro (optional)

PREPARATION

  • Mix flour, semolina, salt, baking powder.
  • Add ghee/oil, water; knead.
  • Rest 30 minutes.
  • Divide into small portions; shape into puris.
  • Fry until crispy.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon tea leaves (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Lemon slices (optional)

PREPARATION

  • Peel and chop ginger into thin slices.
  • In a pot, boil water and add ginger.
  • Reduce heat; simmer 5-7 minutes.
  • Strain tea into cups.
  • Add tea leaves (if using); steep 2-3 minutes.
  • Add honey (if using); stir.
  • Serve hot with lemon slices (if desired).
