Set Dosa

INGREDIENTS

  • Idli rice - 4 cups
  • Urad dal - 1 cup
  • Javvarisi - 1/5 cup
  • Fenugreek - 1 tbsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

PREPARATION

  • Wash and Soak rice, urad dal, sago with fenugreek separately for 6 hours.
  • Drain water, put soaked ingredients in a mixie jar, grind to smooth batter.
  • Add salt and leave the batter for fermentation [approximately. 8 hours].
  • Grease thatte idli plates with gingelly oil.
  • Pour thatte idly batter. Then steam it for 15 min.
  • Serve hot with coconut chutney and Sambar.

INGREDIENTS

  • Leftover idli - 2 to 3
  • Schezwan Sauce - 2 to 3 tbsp
  • Rice flour - 3 tbsp
  • Gram flour- 4 tbsp
  • Asafoetida - ¼ tsp
  • Curry leaves - 1 spring [chopped]
  • Coriander leaves - 2 spings [chopped]
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Water as needed [3 to 4 tbsp]

PREPARATION

  • Cut idli into cubes.
  • In a bowl add idli cubes, schezwan sauce, gram flour, rice flour, salt, coriander leaves, curry leaves, asafoetida, mix well
  • Sprinkle 3 tbsp water, mix well.
  • Heat oil in a pan, sprinkle idli cubes, deep fry till crisp & golden brown on medium hot oil.
  • Once its ready remove from oil & drain on absorbent paper.
  • Serve hot with tea or coffee

INGREDIENTS

  • Mini sorghum idli [Solam idli, jowar idli, jolada idli] – 1 cup [10 idli]
  • Onion – 1 [chopped]
  • Green capsicum – 2 tbsp [chopped]
  • Ginger – 1 tbsp [finely chopped]
  • Garlic – 1 tbsp [finely chopped]
  • Tomato – 1 [chopped], Curry leaves – 1 spring, Coriander leaves – 1 spring
  • Turmeric powder – ¼ tsp, Red chilli powder – ½ tsp, Coriander, cumin & pepper powder – 1 tbsp
  • Garam masala – 1 tsp
  • Oil – 2 tbsp
  • Salt to taste

PREPARATION

  • Heat oil in a pan add mustard seeds & let it splutter. Then add curry leaves sauté.
  • Add onion & capsicum sauté well. Then add ginger & garlic sauté
  • Add tomato sauté till soft. Add water if required.
  • Add salt, turmeric powder, red chilli powder mix well.
  • Add cumin, coriander, pepper powder & garam masala powder sauté.
  • Add mini sola idli mix well.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup Dosa batter
  • ½ cup grated cheese
  • Oil or butter for cooking

PREPARATION

  • Heat pan: Place a pan on medium heat.
  • Spread batter: Pour the batter and spread it thinly.
  • Add cheese: Sprinkle grated cheese on top.
  • Cook: Let the dosa get crispy, and cheese melt.
  • Fold and serve: Fold the dosa in half and serve hot with chutney.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup Dosa batter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil for cooking

PREPARATION

  • Heat pan: Place a pan on medium heat.
  • Spread batter: Pour batter and spread thinly.
  • Add egg: Crack an egg on top, spread it evenly.
  • Season: Add onions, salt, and pepper.
  • Cook: Let it cook until crispy.
  • Fold and serve: Fold the dosa and serve with chutney.
