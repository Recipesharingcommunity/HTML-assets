INGREDIENTS

  • Fish (1 lb)
  • Onions (2)
  • Garlic (2)
  • Tomato (1)
  • Ginger paste (1 tsp)
  • Curry powder (1 tbsp)
  • Turmeric (1 tsp)
  • Salt
  • Oil (2 tbsp)
  • Water (2 cups)

PREPARATION

  • Cut fish into pieces.
  • Fry onions and garlic.
  • Add spices and tomato.
  • Add fish and water; cook 10-12 min.
  • Serve with rice.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup split red lentils (toor dal)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 potato, chopped
  • 1 drumstick (or okra)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (optional)
  • Salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Mustard seeds and curry leaves for tempering

PREPARATION

  • Cook lentils in water until soft.
  • Sauté onions, tomatoes, carrot, potato, and drumstick in oil.
  • Add cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder (if using), and salt.
  • Mix cooked lentils with vegetable mixture.
  • Temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb boneless chicken
  • 2 medium onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder (optional)
  • Salt
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Fresh cilantro

PREPARATION

  • Marinate chicken in yogurt, spices (cumin, coriander, garam masala).
  • Grill or cook chicken until done.
  • Sauté onions, garlic, tomatoes in butter.
  • Add spices, tomato puree, broth.
  • Add cooked chicken, simmer 10-15 min.

INGREDIENTS

  • 250 gm paneer (Indian cheese), cut into cubes
  • 2 medium onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder (optional)
  • Salt
  • 2 tbsp butter/ghee
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 cup water
  • Fresh cilantro

PREPARATION

  • Sauté onions, garlic, tomatoes in butter/ghee.
  • Add cumin, coriander, garam masala, turmeric, chili powder.
  • Add tomato puree, water, salt
  • Add paneer cubes, simmer 10-15 min.
  • Garnish with cilantro.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pound prawns (shrimp), peeled and deveined
  • 2 medium onions, 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 medium tomato, 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder (optional)
  • Salt
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 cup coconut milk (or water)
  • Fresh cilantro

PREPARATION

  • Sauté onions, garlic, tomato in oil.
  • Add cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder.
  • Add prawns, salt, cook 2-3 min.
  • Add coconut milk (or water), simmer 5-7 min.
  • Garnish with cilantro.
